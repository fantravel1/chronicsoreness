Ergonomics

Setting up your environment to reduce pain

What Is Ergonomics?

Ergonomics is the science of designing and arranging things so people can interact with them more efficiently and safely. Good ergonomics can prevent pain and reduce existing symptoms.1

Office/Computer Setup2

Chair

  • Feet flat on floor or footrest
  • Knees at 90Â° angle
  • Lumbar support in lower back curve
  • Armrests support forearms

Desk and Monitor

  • Monitor at arm's length
  • Top of screen at or below eye level
  • Keyboard and mouse at elbow height
  • Documents near screen

Keyboard and Mouse

  • Wrists in neutral position (not bent)
  • Shoulders relaxed
  • Consider ergonomic keyboard/mouse
  • Use keyboard shortcuts

Movement and Breaks

  • Stand or move every 30-60 minutes
  • Microbreaks every 20 minutes
  • Vary postures throughout day
  • Consider sit-stand desk
  • Stretch regularly

Body Mechanics3

Lifting

  • Bend at knees, not waist
  • Keep object close to body
  • Avoid twisting while lifting
  • Know your limits

Standing

  • Weight evenly distributed
  • Knees slightly bent
  • Anti-fatigue mat if prolonged

Sleep Ergonomics

  • Supportive mattress
  • Pillow that maintains neck alignment
  • Consider body pillow for side sleeping
  • Avoid stomach sleeping
