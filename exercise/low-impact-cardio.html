Low-Impact Cardio

Aerobic exercise that's kind to your body

Why Low-Impact Exercise?

Low-impact cardio provides the cardiovascular benefits of aerobic exercise without the jarring forces that can aggravate joints and pain conditions. It's an excellent option for people with arthritis, back pain, or anyone starting to exercise after being sedentary.1

Benefits of Cardio for Pain2

  • Releases endorphins (natural painkillers)
  • Improves mood
  • Better sleep quality
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Weight management
  • Increased energy
  • Improved overall health

Low-Impact Options3

Water-Based

  • Swimming
  • Water aerobics
  • Water walking

Machine-Based

  • Elliptical trainer
  • Stationary bike
  • Recumbent bike
  • Rowing machine

Other Options

  • Walking
  • Cycling (outdoor)
  • Tai chi
  • Low-impact aerobics

Getting Started

  • Start with 5-10 minutes
  • Exercise at conversational pace
  • Build up gradually
  • Goal: 150 minutes moderate activity per week
  • Can be broken into shorter sessions

Intensity Guidelines

  • Should be able to talk but not sing
  • Slight breathlessness is okay
  • Rate of perceived exertion 4-6 out of 10
  • Monitor how you feel during and after

Tips for Success

  • Choose activities you enjoy
  • Warm up and cool down
  • Stay hydrated
  • Listen to your body
  • Consistency matters more than intensity
