Chair Exercises

Effective workouts while seated

Why Chair Exercises?

Chair exercises allow people with chronic pain, mobility limitations, or balance concerns to stay active and build strength safely. Sitting provides stability and reduces stress on painful joints. Chair-based workouts can include stretching, strengthening, and even cardiovascular exerciseâ€”all while seated.1

Benefits2

  • Accessible for limited mobility
  • Reduces fall risk
  • Takes pressure off joints
  • Maintains fitness safely
  • Can be done anywhere
  • Adaptable to many ability levels

Getting Started

  • Use sturdy chair without wheels
  • Sit with feet flat on floor
  • Good posture (sit tall)
  • Clear space around you
  • Wear comfortable clothes

Upper Body Exercises3

Shoulder Rolls

  • Roll shoulders forward and back
  • Relieves tension

Overhead Reach

  • Raise arms overhead
  • Hold briefly, lower

Arm Circles

  • Extend arms to sides
  • Small circles forward and back

Bicep Curls

  • With light weights or cans
  • Curl toward shoulders

Lower Body Exercises

Seated Marching

  • Lift knees alternately
  • Can increase speed for cardio

Leg Extensions

  • Straighten one leg
  • Hold, lower slowly

Ankle Circles

  • Rotate ankles both directions
  • Good for circulation

Heel/Toe Raises

  • Lift heels, then toes
  • Strengthens lower legs

Core Exercises

  • Seated twists (gentle rotation)
  • Abdominal bracing
  • Pelvic tilts
  • Seated crunches

Stretches

  • Neck stretches (tilt side to side)
  • Chest opener
  • Seated forward bend
  • Seated figure-4 stretch
  • Wrist stretches

Chair Cardio

  • Seated jumping jacks (modified)
  • Fast marching
  • Punching motions
  • Can raise heart rate

Tips for Success

  • Start slowly
  • Breathe normally
  • Stop if pain increases
  • Progress gradually
  • Aim for consistency
  • Many YouTube videos available
