Aquatic Therapy

Healing in the water

Why Water Works

Aquatic therapy uses the unique properties of waterâ€”buoyancy, resistance, and warmthâ€”to make exercise easier and more comfortable for people with chronic pain. Water supports body weight, reduces joint stress, and allows movement that might be too painful on land. It's one of the most recommended exercise forms for chronic pain conditions.1

Properties of Water2

Buoyancy

  • Reduces body weight by up to 90%
  • Less stress on joints
  • Allows movement without pain
  • Supports weakened muscles

Resistance

  • Natural resistance for strengthening
  • Adjustable by speed of movement
  • Works muscles in all directions

Warmth

  • Warm water relaxes muscles
  • Reduces pain and stiffness
  • Improves circulation
  • Therapeutic pools typically 84-94Â°F

Conditions That Benefit3

  • Arthritis (osteo and rheumatoid)
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Chronic back pain
  • Joint replacements
  • Chronic fatigue syndrome
  • Obesity-related pain
  • Balance problems
  • Post-surgical recovery

Types of Aquatic Exercise

Aquatic Physical Therapy

  • One-on-one with therapist
  • Targeted to your condition
  • Often covered by insurance

Water Aerobics Classes

  • Group exercise in pool
  • Arthritis-specific classes available
  • Social and fun

Swimming

  • Good cardiovascular exercise
  • Freestyle and backstroke often best
  • May need technique modifications

Water Walking

  • Simple and effective
  • Walk in chest-deep water
  • Good starting point

Getting Started

  • Ask about therapeutic pools
  • Check local YMCAs, community centers
  • Arthritis Foundation aquatic programs
  • Hospital-based pools
  • Start slowly

Tips for Success

  • Warm pool temperature important
  • Use ramp or steps (not ladder)
  • Water shoes for grip
  • Stay hydrated (you still sweat)
  • Listen to your body
  • Consistency matters

Considerations

  • Some have chlorine sensitivity
  • Hot tubs may worsen some conditions
  • Infection precautions with wounds
  • Transportation to pool needed
