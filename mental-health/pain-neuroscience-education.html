What Is Pain Neuroscience Education?

Pain neuroscience education (PNE) is an approach that teaches people the biology and neuroscience of pain. Pain Is a Brain Output

  • Not simply a measure of tissue damage
  • Brain decides if you "need" pain
  • Purpose is protection

Why Education Helps3

  • Changes unhelpful beliefs
  • Reduces threat perception
  • Empowers self-management
  • Can actually reduce pain intensity
  • Improves treatment outcomes

  • Reduces disability
  • Changes pain beliefs
  • Works best combined with movement
  • Evidence continues to grow
