Cyclobenzaprine (Flexeril)

Muscle relaxant for spasm relief

What Is Cyclobenzaprine?

Cyclobenzaprine (brand name Flexeril) is a muscle relaxant used to relieve muscle spasms and associated pain. It works in the brain rather than directly on muscles. It's typically used for short-term treatment of acute musculoskeletal conditions.1

How It Works

  • Acts in the central nervous system
  • Reduces muscle hyperactivity
  • Related to tricyclic antidepressants
  • Doesn't work directly on muscles

Uses2

  • Acute muscle spasms
  • Back and neck muscle pain
  • Short-term musculoskeletal conditions
  • Typically part of comprehensive treatment

Dosing

  • Immediate-release: 5-10mg three times daily
  • Extended-release: 15-30mg once daily
  • Usually prescribed for 2-3 weeks maximum
  • Lower doses often as effective, fewer side effects

Side Effects3

Common

  • Drowsiness (most common)
  • Dry mouth
  • Dizziness
  • Fatigue
  • Constipation
  • Blurred vision

Less Common

  • Confusion
  • Urinary retention
  • Rapid heartbeat

Precautions

  • Don't use with MAOIs
  • Caution in elderly (more sensitive)
  • Avoid with heart conditions
  • Don't combine with alcohol
  • May impair driving/operating machinery
  • Not for long-term use

Tips for Use

  • Take at bedtime if drowsiness is issue
  • Lower dose may work with fewer side effects
  • Combine with rest, ice/heat, stretching
  • Not meant for long-term muscle pain
