Hand and Wrist Pain

Understanding chronic hand problems

Importance of Hand Function

Our hands are essential for daily activities, work, and communication. Chronic hand pain can significantly impact quality of life and independence. Many conditions can cause hand pain, each requiring specific approaches.1

Common Causes2

Arthritis

  • Osteoarthritis (base of thumb, finger joints)
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Psoriatic arthritis

Nerve Problems

  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Cubital tunnel syndrome
  • Peripheral neuropathy

Tendon Problems

  • De Quervain's tendinopathy
  • Trigger finger
  • Tendinitis

Other

  • Ganglion cysts
  • Dupuytren's contracture
  • CRPS

When to Seek Care

  • Persistent pain affecting function
  • Numbness or weakness
  • Visible deformity
  • Signs of infection
  • Symptoms worsening despite self-care

General Treatment Approaches3

Conservative

  • Splinting
  • Hand therapy
  • NSAIDs, topical treatments
  • Activity modification
  • Ergonomic changes

Interventional

  • Corticosteroid injections
  • Trigger point injections

Surgical

  • Carpal tunnel release
  • Trigger finger release
  • Joint replacement/fusion

Protecting Your Hands

  • Use larger joints when possible
  • Avoid tight grip
  • Take breaks from repetitive tasks
  • Use adaptive tools
