About ChronicSoreness

Empowering women with evidence-based information to understand and manage chronic pain conditions.

Our Mission

ChronicSoreness was created with a simple but powerful purpose: to provide women with the accurate, compassionate information they need to navigate chronic pain conditions.

We believe that knowledge is power. When you understand your condition, you can advocate for yourself, make informed treatment decisions, and take meaningful steps toward improving your quality of life.

Why This Matters

Chronic pain affects millions of women worldwide, yet:

  • Women's pain is often dismissed or attributed to psychological factors
  • Diagnosis takes an average of 7+ years for conditions like endometriosis
  • Research has historically underrepresented women, leaving gaps in understanding
  • Misinformation online can lead to ineffective or harmful treatments
  • Isolation and lack of support compound the suffering of chronic illness

ChronicSoreness exists to bridge these gaps—providing reliable information, validating experiences, and connecting women with the resources they need.

Our Values

Evidence-Based

We base our content on peer-reviewed research, clinical guidelines, and expert consensus. When evidence is limited or conflicting, we say so.

Patient-Centered

We respect that you are the expert on your own body. Our goal is to inform and empower, not to tell you what to do.

Compassionate

We understand that living with chronic pain is hard. We strive to provide information with empathy and without judgment.

Inclusive

Chronic pain affects women of all backgrounds. We aim to create resources that are accessible and relevant to diverse experiences.

Transparent

We clearly identify our sources, acknowledge limitations, and distinguish between established facts and emerging research.

Supportive

We believe no one should face chronic pain alone. We connect readers with communities and resources for ongoing support.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

Content Development Process

All content on ChronicSoreness goes through a rigorous process:

  1. Research: Content is developed using peer-reviewed medical literature, clinical guidelines, and reputable health organizations
  2. Writing: Information is presented in clear, accessible language without oversimplifying complex topics
  3. Review: Content is reviewed for accuracy and balanced perspective
  4. Updates: We regularly review and update content as new research emerges

Sources We Trust

  • Peer-reviewed medical journals
  • National Institutes of Health (NIH)
  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
  • Major medical associations (AMA, ACR, ACOG, etc.)
  • Cochrane Reviews
  • UpToDate and other clinical decision resources
