B Vitamins and Pain

Essential nutrients for nerve health

Why B Vitamins Matter

B vitamins play crucial roles in nerve function, energy production, and nervous system health. Deficiencies in certain B vitamins can cause or worsen neuropathy and other pain conditions. While not a pain treatment per se, ensuring adequate B vitamin status may support overall pain management.1

Key B Vitamins for Pain2

Vitamin B12 (Cobalamin)

  • Essential for nerve myelin
  • Deficiency causes neuropathy
  • Numbness, tingling, burning
  • Common deficiency in older adults
  • Metformin can cause deficiency

Vitamin B1 (Thiamine)

  • Important for nerve function
  • Deficiency common in alcoholism
  • Can cause painful neuropathy

Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine)

  • Nerve function and neurotransmitters
  • Deficiency can cause neuropathy
  • Excess can also cause neuropathy
  • Balance is important

Folate (B9)

  • Works with B12
  • Nervous system development
  • DNA synthesis

Signs of B Vitamin Deficiency3

  • Numbness and tingling
  • Burning sensations
  • Balance problems
  • Fatigue
  • Cognitive changes
  • Mood changes
  • Mouth sores

Who's at Risk for Deficiency

  • Older adults (B12)
  • Vegetarians/vegans (B12)
  • Heavy alcohol users (B1)
  • Gastric bypass patients
  • Certain medication users
  • Crohn's disease, celiac disease

Testing

  • Blood tests for B12, folate
  • Methylmalonic acid (MMA) more sensitive for B12
  • Homocysteine levels
  • Ask doctor if concerned

Food Sources

B12

  • Meat, fish, poultry
  • Eggs and dairy
  • Fortified foods
  • Not naturally in plants

B1

  • Whole grains
  • Pork
  • Legumes
  • Nuts and seeds

B6

  • Poultry, fish
  • Potatoes
  • Bananas
  • Chickpeas

Supplementation

  • Test before supplementing high doses
  • B12 sublingual or injection if absorption issues
  • Don't exceed safe upper limits (especially B6)
  • B-complex provides balance

Cautions

  • B6 toxicity causes neuropathy (high doses)
  • B12 supplements can mask folate deficiency
  • Consult healthcare provider
